# Cambium Academy

Free, beginner-friendly AI courses from the Cambium AI Research Institution. A new course every week. No math, no code, no signup, no payment.

**Start here:** https://pkjaslam.github.io/cambium-academy/start/

Every course follows the same learning path: watch the lecture, clear all the flashcards, explore the hands-on AI Lab, and the quiz unlocks. Score 70% or better and your certificate prints on the spot, ready for LinkedIn.
