Module 1 · The lesson

Finding real, citable sources with AI

AI can find sources in seconds. It can also invent ones that look perfectly real. This lesson is about using AI to find sources you can actually trust and cite, and spotting the fakes.

  • Use search-grounded tools like Perplexity or Gemini Deep Research that cite as they answer, rather than a plain chatbot that answers from memory.
  • Open every citation. AI sometimes fabricates plausible-looking references, so a link you never click is a link you cannot trust.
  • Ask for primary sources and direct links, not a summary of a summary.
  • Cross-check key facts in a second, independent source before you rely on them. That habit is called lateral reading.
