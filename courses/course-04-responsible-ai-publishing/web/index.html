The question has changed

"Is the science sound? Are the data authentic? Are the conclusions supported? And was the AI use disclosed honestly?"
Nearly every researcher now uses AI somewhere, and the tools that claim to detect it are unreliable and biased: seven detectors flagged 61.3% of non-native English speakers' essays as AI-generated while scoring near-perfectly on native speakers. So "was this written with AI?" is both unanswerable and cruel. The four questions above are answerable, and this course teaches you to answer them from all three chairs: author, reviewer, editor. No moral panic. No scolding honest researchers. Just the 2026 rules, the real red flags, and a spine.
