## A note on freshness

Prompting advice ages fast: half of what you will find online was true in 2023 and is wrong now. The resources above were chosen because they are maintained and vendor-official or research-linked, so they stay current. Course facts and model names are a July 2026 snapshot (checked 2026-07-09); the durable ideas (tiers, modes, structure, showing an example, and verifying answers) are what to hold onto. The full source list for the lecture's claims lives inline in `course-content.md` (Section 7, Fact base summary).
