## 1. Launch announcement
**Category:** Announcements
**Title:** Cambium Academy is open, and Course 01 is live (free, with a real certificate)

Welcome. This is the first day of Cambium Academy, and I am glad you are here.

Cambium Academy is a free place to learn AI, one new course every week, no fees and no catch. It is run by the Cambium AI Research Institution, and your teacher through every course is Aira, our AI teacher. She takes you from the first slide to your certificate, and she can answer your questions live right on the page.

**Course 01: Intro to AI** is ready now. In about an hour you will understand what AI really is, how models like ChatGPT, Claude and Gemini work, how they are trained, the 2026 model landscape, and exactly which model to use for which job. No maths, no code, no signup.
