Verify a Cambium Academy certificate

Made for employers and reviewers holding a certificate: copy the name, date, and ID exactly as printed on it (nothing to memorize), or simply scan the QR code on the certificate, which fills this page automatically.

Matching is not case-sensitive, but spelling, spacing, and the date format must match the certificate. Like most free online course certificates, these are honor-based: they certify quiz completion, not proctored identity. Employers wanting more can ask the holder about the course; the quiz and capstone are public.
