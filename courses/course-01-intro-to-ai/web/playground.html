LAB 05 · BONUS

Word math

Inside a model, words are points in space, and meaning is geometry. The most famous trick in AI: take king, subtract man, add woman, and the nearest word is queen. Watch it happen.

Real models place words in a space of thousands of dimensions; this map is a two-dimensional sketch of the same idea. It is why models grasp analogy, tone, and relationship, not just spelling.
