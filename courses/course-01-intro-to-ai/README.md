## Learning outcomes

By the end, a learner can explain what AI is in one sentence, describe how a language model works (tokens, weights, attention) without math, walk through the four training steps and explain hallucination, name the major models of July 2026 and the closed versus open weights split, and pick a sensible model for writing, coding, research, documents, media, or private work.

## Modules

1. **What is AI?** Definition, the AI to generative AI family tree, 75 years of history, AI already in daily life. (10 min)
2. **How AI models work.** Next-word prediction, tokens, neural networks as billions of tuned dials, the transformer and attention, context windows. (12 min)
