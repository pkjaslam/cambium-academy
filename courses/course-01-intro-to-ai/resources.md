# Course 01 · Free learning resources

Every link below was verified live and free on July 8, 2026 by Cambium's librarian pass. If one breaks, open an issue on the repo.

## Start here (under one hour each)

| Resource | Link | Format | Time | Why it's worth it |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| How Machines Learn (CGP Grey) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R9OHn5ZF4Uo | Video | 5 min | The best five-minute mental model for how a machine learns |
| Intro to Large Language Models (Andrej Karpathy) | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zjkBMFhNj_g | Video | 1 hr | A legendary plain-language talk by a top AI researcher |
| Neural Networks (3Blue1Brown) | https://www.3blue1brown.com/topics/neural-networks | Video series | 1 hr core | The best visual intuition ever made for neural nets |
| Introduction to Generative AI (Google Cloud) | https://www.cloudskillsboost.google/course_templates/536 | Course | 45 min | Fastest structured on-ramp to generative AI |

## Structured courses (free)

| Resource | Link | Format | Time | Level |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Elements of AI (University of Helsinki) | https://www.elementsofai.com/ | Interactive course | ~25 hrs self-paced | Beginner, no math or code, 2M+ learners |
| AI For Everyone (Andrew Ng, DeepLearning.AI) | https://www.coursera.org/learn/ai-for-everyone | Course, free to audit | ~10 hrs | Beginner, built for non-engineers |
| Anthropic Academy: AI Fluency | https://anthropic.skilljar.com/ | Interactive course + certificate | 1 to 3 hrs per course | Beginner track available, free certificate |
| Google Machine Learning Crash Course | https://developers.google.com/machine-learning/crash-course | Course | ~15 hrs | Beginner to intermediate, some code shown |
| Prompt Engineering Guide (DAIR.AI) | https://www.promptingguide.ai/ | Interactive guide | 1 to 2 hrs core | The most-cited free reference for prompting well |
