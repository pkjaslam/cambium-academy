## Adding a new course

1. Duplicate a course block above, bump the number and slug.
2. Fill in the course page and lecture-video links.
3. Build a `social-courseNN.png` share card and point that course's og:image at it.
4. Flip the `/start/` catalog card from "coming soon" to LIVE and link it in.
5. Update this file, then push.
